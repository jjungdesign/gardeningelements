    About

    A garden is a space, set aside for the cultivation, and enjoyment of plants and other forms of nature. There's is no rules for creating a personal garden– it is an expression of taste or philosophy of a community or indivisual. Often private garden is created with whatever plants are at hand or strike our fancy. When your garden starts to feel chaotic, there are some key elements that make a garden feel more cohesive.
